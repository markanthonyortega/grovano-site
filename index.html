Residential real estate, bought and sold directly.

Grovano Inc is a residential real estate investment company. We purchase homes directly from owners for cash, and we connect investors with off-market investment properties.

What we do

  • For sellers. We make cash offers on residential property, handle the walkthrough and title work, and close on the seller's timeline without listing, showings, or repairs.
  • For buyers and investors. We source off-market and value-add residential properties and share deals that match an investor's stated criteria, budget, and target areas.

Our text messaging program

Grovano Inc uses SMS to communicate with clients and leads who have asked us to text them. Messages are conversational and transaction-related: cash offer confirmations and details, follow-up questions about a property, walkthrough and showing scheduling, closing status updates, and property alerts matching criteria an investor gave us.

We only text people who have given us express written or verbal consent. We never buy, rent, or otherwise obtain phone numbers from lead lists or third parties.
