SMS opt-in verification

This page exists so that carrier and messaging reviewers can verify the Grovano Inc SMS opt-in flow without any barriers.

The opt-in form is fully public. It is not behind a login, account wall, paywall, or client portal. Any reviewer can open it directly in a browser and see the checkbox area.

Live opt-in form:

https://grovano.com/messaging/signup

Consent is collected only in writing. The form presents two separate consent checkboxes, both unchecked by default. Transactional consent and marketing consent are never combined. A reproduction of the exact checkbox area is shown below.

Reproduction of the live opt-in checkbox area at grovano.com/messaging/signup
Checkbox 1 of 2
1. Transaction updates (optional) Informational and transactional
By checking this box, I agree to receive text messages from Grovano Inc at the mobile number I provided about my own transaction: confirmation of my cash offer request, cash offer details, follow-up questions about my property, walkthrough and showing scheduling, and closing status updates. Consent is not a condition of purchase or sale. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time, or HELP for help.Unchecked by default
Checkbox 2 of 2 — separate consent
2. Property and deal alerts (optional) Marketing
Separately, by checking this box, I agree to receive marketing text messages from Grovano Inc about investment properties and deals matching my criteria, including new listings and price changes. This is a separate consent from the box above and is not required. Consent is not a condition of purchase or sale. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time, or HELP for help.Unchecked by default

The checkboxes above are shown disabled because this is a verification reproduction. To use the actual working form, open https://grovano.com/messaging/signup. Program details are in the SMS Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy, which states that no mobile information or SMS opt-in data is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
