SMS Terms and Conditions

Last updated: July 19, 2026

These Terms and Conditions govern the Grovano Inc text messaging program (the "Program"). By opting in, you agree to these terms. Grovano Inc ("Grovano," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Program.

1. Program description

Grovano Inc sends text messages to clients and leads who have expressly opted in. The Program has two separate message categories, and you consent to each one separately:

  • Transaction updates (informational and transactional). Messages about your own transaction: confirmation of your cash offer request, cash offer details, follow-up questions about your property, walkthrough and showing scheduling, and closing status updates.
  • Property and deal alerts (marketing). Messages about investment properties and deals matching criteria you gave us, including new listings and price changes.

Consenting to one category does not consent you to the other. You may join either, both, or neither. The Program is two-way: you can reply with questions or to schedule, and our team responds directly.

2. How to opt in

All consent for this Program is collected in writing. We do not accept verbal consent for marketing messages. You may join by either of the following methods:

  • Website form. Submit the form at grovano.com/messaging/signup. The page presents two separate consent checkboxes, both unchecked by default, one for transaction updates and one for marketing property and deal alerts. You may check either, both, or neither.
  • Text-in keyword. Text our business number first to start a conversation, or reply START, YES, or SUBSCRIBE.

After you opt in, you will receive a confirmation message: "Grovano Inc: You're now opted in to receive property and cash offer updates. Msg frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, STOP to opt out."

Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchase, sale, or of receiving an offer from Grovano Inc.

3. Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on your activity and the stage of your transaction.

4. Cost

Grovano Inc does not charge for the Program. Message and data rates may apply. Messages sent and received count toward the plan you have with your wireless carrier. Contact your carrier for details about your plan.

5. How to opt out

You may cancel at any time by replying STOP, OPTOUT, CANCEL, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE, REVOKE, or STOPALL to any message from us. You will receive one confirmation message: "You have successfully been unsubscribed. You will not receive any more messages from this number. Reply START to resubscribe." After that, we will send no further messages to that number unless you opt back in.

6. How to get help

Reply HELP or INFO to any message from us and you will receive: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Msg&Data Rates May Apply." You can also reach us at support@grovano.com or (210) 347-9018.

7. Supported carriers

The Program is available on major U.S. wireless carriers, including AT&T, Verizon Wireless, T-Mobile, US Cellular, and their affiliates and MVNOs. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

8. Delivery and device requirements

Message delivery depends on your carrier's network and your device. Grovano Inc does not guarantee that messages will be delivered, timely, or error free, and is not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. You must have a text-capable mobile device and an active wireless plan to participate.

9. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and the account holder of, or have authority to consent for, the mobile number you provide. You agree to notify us if you give up or change that number so that we do not send messages to a subsequent holder.

10. Privacy

Your information is handled according to our Privacy Policy. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Grovano Inc does not sell, rent, or trade mobile numbers or SMS opt-in data.

11. Changes to these terms

We may modify or cancel the Program or these Terms at any time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes affecting subscribers, notify active participants.
